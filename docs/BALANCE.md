# Game Balance Notes

## Weapon Stats

| Weapon | Damage | Fire Rate | Reload | Range |
|--------|--------|-----------|--------|-------|
| Pistol | 25 | Medium | Fast | Short |
| Rifle | 35 | Fast | Medium | Medium |
| Sniper | 80 | Slow | Slow | Long |

## Enemy Difficulty Scaling

- Level 1-5: Basic drones
- Level 6-10: Shielded enemies
- Level 11-15: Boss encounters
- Level 16+: Infinite scaling

## Performance Targets

- 60 FPS on mid-range devices
- Load time < 3 seconds
- Memory usage < 200MB
